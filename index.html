Stress management survey on students in Indonesia

This survey aims to understand students experiences of stress, the factors that contribute to higher stress levels, and effective stress management strategies. This survey can be carried out using a questionnaire designed to assess the level of stress experienced by students and the factors that influence it.

Comparative survey of the use of Tokopedia and Shopee markertpalce with the Mann Whitney Test

A study that aims to determine the significant differences between using the Tokopedia and Shopee marketplaces on shopping decisions. The method used in this research is a survey and Mann-Whitney non-parametric statistical test

Configure the MikroTik as an internet gateway

Mikrotik RouterOS is a router-based operating system that has various features and functions to organize and manage networks. In learning about the configuration of Mikrotik things that are learned starting from including how to configure network interfaces, routing, firewalls, NAT (Network Address Translation), DHCP (Dynamic Host Configuration Protocol), and DNS (Domain Name System). In addition, students must also understand basic concepts about networks, such as network topology, network protocols, and subnetting.
